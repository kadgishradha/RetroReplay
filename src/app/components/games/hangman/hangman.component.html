Rules of Word Guess
  1. Objective: Guess the hidden word by selecting letters one at a time.
  2. Gameplay: You will be presented with a hidden word represented by dashes, each dash representing a letter in the word. Guess the letters one by one. If the guessed letter is in the word, it will be revealed in its corresponding position(s). If the letter is not in the word, you will receive a strike.
  3. Strikes: You have a limited number of incorrect guesses before the game ends. Once you reach the maximum number of strikes, the game is over.
  4. Winning: If you successfully guess all the letters in the word before accumulating too many strikes, you win the game.
  5. Hint: Some games may offer hints or categories to help you guess the word. Use them wisely!
  6. Play Again: After completing a game, you have the option to play again and try to beat your previous score.
  7. Have Fun: Word Guess is a fun and challenging game. Enjoy the process of unraveling words and expanding your vocabulary!
