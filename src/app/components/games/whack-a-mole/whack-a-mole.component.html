Rules of Whack a Mole
  1. Objective: Your objective is to whack as many moles as possible within the given time frame.
  2. Gameplay: Click on the moles as they pop up from their holes. Each successful hit earns you points.
  3. Scoring: You earn 10 points for each successful mole whack.
  4. Bombs: Be careful not to click on bombs! Each bomb clicked will deduct 5 points from your score.
  5. Duration: The game lasts for a set duration of 30 seconds. You must score as many points as possible within this time frame.
  6. Game Over: When the time runs out, the game ends. Your final score will be displayed on the screen.
  7. Play Again: You can play the game again to beat your high score. Click the "Play Again" button to start a new game.
