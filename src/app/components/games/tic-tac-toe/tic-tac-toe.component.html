Rules of Tic Tac Toe
  1. Objective: The game is played on a 3x3 grid. The objective is to be the first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of your symbols (X or O) in a row.
  2. Players: The game is typically played by two players, who take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in an empty cell of the grid.
  3. Starting the Game: The game starts with an empty grid.
  4. Taking Turns: Players take turns alternately. The player using X usually goes first.
  5. Placing Symbols: On your turn, you place your symbol (X or O) into an empty cell of the grid.
  6. Winning: If a player succeeds in placing three of their symbols in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row, that player wins the game.
  7. Draw (Tie): If all cells are filled and no player has formed a winning line, the game is a draw (tie).
