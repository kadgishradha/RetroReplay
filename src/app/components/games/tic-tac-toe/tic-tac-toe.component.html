Rules of Tic Tac Toe
  1. Game Board: The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Players: The game is played between two players. One player uses 'X' markers, and the other player uses 'O' markers.
  3. Objective: The objective of the game is to be the first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three markers (X's or O's) on the grid.
  4. Turns: Players take turns placing their markers on empty cells of the grid.
  5. Starting Player: The player using 'X' markers typically starts the game.
  6. Gameplay: On their turn, each player places their marker ('X' or 'O') on an empty cell of the grid. Players continue taking turns until one player wins or the game ends in a draw.
  7. Winning: A player wins the game if they successfully create a line of three of their markers (X's or O's) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game ends immediately when a player achieves this.
  8. Draw: If all cells on the grid are filled with markers, and no player has formed a line of three, the game ends in a draw.
  9. Replay: After the game ends (either in a win or draw), players have the option to replay the game if they wish to continue playing.
