Rules of Connect Four
  1. Objective: Connect Four is a two-player strategy game where the goal is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own discs.
  2. Setup: The game is played on a vertical grid board with 6 rows and 7 columns. Each player is assigned a color, usually red or yellow.
  3. Turns: Players take turns dropping a colored disc into one of the columns. The discs fall down to the lowest unoccupied position within the selected column.
  4. Winning Condition: The game is won by the player who successfully connects four of their own colored discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. Vertical Win: A player wins if they connect four of their discs vertically in a column.
  6. Horizontal Win: A player wins if they connect four of their discs horizontally in a row.
  7. Diagonal Win: A player wins if they connect four of their discs diagonally along a slope.
  8. Draw: If all columns are filled without either player achieving a winning condition, the game ends in a draw.
