Rules of Connect Four
  1. Game Board: The game is played on a vertical grid with 7 columns and 6 rows.
  2. Players: The game is played between two players. One player uses red markers, and the other player uses yellow markers.
  3. Objective: The objective of the game is to be the first player to connect four of their markers (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) in a straight line.
  4. Starting Player: The player using red markers typically starts the game.
  5. Turns: Players take turns dropping their markers into any column of the grid. The marker falls to the lowest available empty cell in the selected column.
  6. Gameplay: On their turn, each player drops one of their markers into any column of the grid. Players continue taking turns until one player wins or the game ends in a draw.
  7. Winning: A player wins the game if they successfully connect four of their markers (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) in a straight line. The game ends immediately when a player achieves this.
  8. Draw: If all cells on the grid are filled with markers, and no player has connected four markers in a row, the game ends in a draw.
  9. Replay: After the game ends (either in a win or draw), players have the option to replay the game if they wish to continue playing.
